Eleven (El)

A young girl with psychokinetic abilities raised in Hawkins Lab. She loves Eggos and will do anything to protect her friends.

Mike Wheeler

The optimistic leader of the group. He is the Dungeon Master and the emotional core who never gave up hope on finding Will.

Will Byers

The boy who vanished. Even after being rescued, he retains a chilling connection to the Mind Flayer and the Upside Down.

Dustin Henderson

The peacekeeper and the brains. A science enthusiast with a great smile who often discovers the monsters' weaknesses first.

Lucas Sinclair

The realist and the Ranger. He is skeptical and pragmatic, armed with his trusty wrist-rocket slingshot.

Jim Hopper

The tough Chief of Police. Once a broken man, he becomes a heroic father figure to Eleven and a defender of Hawkins.

Joyce Byers

Will and Jonathan's fierce mother. She is relentless, intuitive, and the first to believe in the Upside Down.

Nancy Wheeler

Mike's older sister and an aspiring journalist. She transforms from a suburban teenager into a sharp shooter and monster hunter.

Jonathan Byers

Will's older brother. An introverted photographer who cares deeply for his family and teams up with Nancy to expose the truth.

Steve Harrington

Former popular jock turned babysitter extraordinaire. Armed with a nail-bat and great hair, he is the brave protector of the younger kids.

Max Mayfield

"Madmax" is a skateboarder and arcade gamer. She becomes a core member of the party and a target for Vecna due to her hidden trauma.

Robin Buckley

Steve's best friend and coworker. She is brilliant, speaks multiple languages, and cracked the Russian code in Season 3.

Erica Sinclair

Lucas's little sister. Sassy, intelligent, and a capitalist at heart, she isn't afraid to crawl through air ducts to save the day.

Henry Creel (001)

The original test subject of Hawkins Lab who became the monstrous Vecna. He controls the hive mind and seeks to destroy humanity.

Holly Wheeler

The youngest Wheeler sibling. Though often in the background, she witnesses the supernatural events unfolding around her family.
