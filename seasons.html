The Timeline

Season 1: The Vanishing

The nightmare begins in 1983 when 12-year-old Will Byers vanishes. His friends meet a psychokinetic girl named Eleven. Together, they uncover "The Upside Down" and the Demogorgon.

Season 2: The Mind Flayer

A year later (1984), Will is plagued by visions of a shadow monster. New student Max arrives. It is revealed that the Mind Flayer is possessing Will to spy on our world.

Season 3: Starcourt Mall

Summer of 1985. The Mind Flayer returns, building a body out of melted flesh. Hopper and Joyce discover a secret Russian base underneath the new Starcourt Mall.

Season 4: The Curse of Vecna

A new threat, Vecna (Henry Creel), kills teenagers in Hawkins. Eleven struggles to regain her powers, and the season ends with the Upside Down merging into Hawkins.

Season 5: The Final Chapter

The final showdown. The group reunites in a destroyed Hawkins for one last stand against Vecna and his army to save the world from total consumption.
